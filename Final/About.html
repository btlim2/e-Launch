Welcome to e-launch!

There are extensive steps involved in setting up a business and launching an online business. In the current market, there are many information presentation platforms directed at presenting generic information to new business owners. Upon researching many different online platforms, you may still find it extremely challenging to gather all necessary information required to make an informed decision.

This is where e-launch comes in! Targeting new online business owners, just like you, we aim to deliver a product that will assist and support your journey to launching your own online business in the following ways:

  • - An interactive timeline will guide new business owners through different steps required to set up their online business. By utilising the interactive timeline, users can track and monitor their progress in regards to launching their business.
  • - Specific to each task on the timeline, detailed information and a list of service provider will be available to the users. Service provider will be recommended based on each user’s unique requirement and preference.
  • - E-launch aims to streamline the process of setting up an online business through the interactive timeline and service provider recommendation functionalities.
